Fresh Bakery

At ASAGOHAN, we celebrate the art of traditional baking. Each day, our pastry team begins early, crafting fresh breads, pastries, and sweets — all made from scratch in our own kitchen. We carefully select high-quality ingredients and apply time-honored techniques to ensure every creation is full of flavor.

From the first morning batch to the last treat of the day, our in-house bakery brings warmth, freshness, and craftsmanship to every bite.

Our Story

Asagohan (朝ごはん) — meaning "breakfast" in Japanese — was born from a love of authentic Japanese comfort food, vibrant Cambodian ingredients, and Western culinary traditions.

Whether you're starting your day before Angkor Wat or winding down after exploring Siem Reap, we invite you to share a meal with us. From delicate onigiri to hearty Khmer Amok, from all-day breakfast plates to aromatic coffee and sophisticated cocktails, we celebrate fusion with respect for tradition.
